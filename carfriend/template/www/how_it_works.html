FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the inspection really free?
Yes. The 200-point inspection is completely free for sellers. CarFriend earns a small platform fee only on successful sales.
How long does the whole process take?
Inspection is typically done within 24 hours of scheduling. Once the report is approved, auctions run for a set duration (usually a few hours). Most sellers complete the full process — inspection to payment — within 48–72 hours.
Can I set a minimum price for my car?
Yes. You can set a reserve price before the auction goes live. The reserve is not disclosed to bidders. If no bid meets your reserve, the auction ends without a sale and you can choose to relist or adjust the reserve.
Do I need to be present during the inspection?
It is recommended but not mandatory. You or a representative with access to the vehicle and documents should be present for the first 10–15 minutes. Our inspector is trained to be efficient.
How do dealers get access to bid?
Dealers register on the Platform, complete KYC verification, and gain access to all live auctions. Registration is free — contact us to get started.
