Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, phone, email, Aadhaar/PAN for KYC), information generated through use of the Platform (auction history, inspection records, transaction data), and technical data (IP address, browser type, device information, cookies).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process KYC verification and prevent fraud
  • To facilitate auctions, payments, and vehicle transfers
  • To send transaction notifications and service updates
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve the Platform

3. KYC Data

KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, driving licence) are used solely for identity verification. We use government-approved verification services. KYC data is stored with AES-256 encryption and is never shared with third parties except as required by law or for fraud prevention.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who operate under data processing agreements (payment gateways, SMS/email services)
  • Government agencies or law enforcement when required by law
  • Auction participants — only the information necessary to complete a transaction

5. Data Retention

Transaction records and KYC data are retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations. You may request deletion of non-essential personal data by contacting us at support@carfriend.in. KYC and transaction records cannot be deleted due to regulatory requirements.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 at rest, regular security audits, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure — please protect your credentials and report any suspected breach immediately.

7. Cookies

We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements). To exercise these rights, contact support@carfriend.in.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact us for immediate deletion.

10. Changes to this Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this Policy via email or a prominent Platform notice at least 7 days before the change takes effect.
