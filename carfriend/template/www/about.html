Our Mission

Transparency at Every Step

India's used car market is plagued by information asymmetry — buyers don't know what they're getting, and sellers don't know if they're getting a fair price.

CarFriend changes this by putting a certified inspection report at the center of every transaction. When both parties have access to the same objective data, trust happens naturally — and deals close faster at better prices.

Our platform combines rigorous inspections, real-time competitive auctions, and dedicated relationship managers to deliver an experience that's genuinely better for everyone involved.
